About

The Rigging Company supports film, television and commercial productions with specialist rigging equipment and on-set expertise.

Founded by Matthew Newton, Callum Clements and Alex Glover, all working Head of Department riggers, the company is built on years of practical experience across studio builds, location work and large-scale lighting rigs.

Alongside equipment supply, the team can provide full HOD rigging services, bringing production knowledge and technical understanding to complex builds.

Our approach is simple — the right equipment, prepared properly, ready for production.
